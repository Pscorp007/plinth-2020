Plinth 2020

Techno-Management Fest, Jaipur

About Us... Plinth is the annual inter college Techno-Management-Literary Festival of the LNMIIT, Jaipur. A bud when comes out of its cover, strives for the glow which could make it an attractive flower. So, here in Plinth as we celebrate the sixth year of its successful organization, we are proud to say that the bud found its glow in the enthusiasm and the confidence of its participants. Plinth brings immense opportunities for all the enthusiasts to showcase their talents and skills. The coalescence of all the different fields of career takes place at various corners of a single place. It starts with a typical morning that brings everyone out of their warm coziness. Ready and suited to make it grand and successful, after the long days of hard work.
